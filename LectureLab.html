Lecture Lab

Branding

LectureLab was the concept for a tutoring platform designed to connect tutors and students. As a new startup they were looking to develop their brand. My role was to create their logo, and brand guidelines that they could later use while developing their interface.

Logo & Guidelines

For the final design they chose two shades of green that were reminiscent of a chalkboard. The logo design was meant to be the first stage in the development of their brand. When I created the final deliverables I created a simple brand guideline, that could be expanded upon when they began the development of their interface.
