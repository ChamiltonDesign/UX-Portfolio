36 Under 36

Editorial

Each year The Jewish Week creates a special issue, that highlights 36 Jewish individuals under the age of 36, making a difference in their community. I was asked to design the cover for the 2020 issue. For this project I collaborated with a fellow designer by using the images she created for the web articles in my cover design.

Process

We worked on this project during the pandemic, and the editorial team wanted to include a special feature for the first responders. I was a little inspired by social distancing, due to working from home at the time, so I really wanted to incorporate this into the design in some way. The editorial team liked the concept and chose a headline for the cover.
